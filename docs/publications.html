Citing CHAP

If you are using CHAP in your own research, please cite the following paper:

Klesse, G., Rao, S., Sansom, M. S., & Tucker, S. J. (2019). CHAP: a versatile tool for the structural and functional annotation of ion channel pores. Journal of molecular biology, 431(17), 3353-3365.

Other Publications Relating to CHAP

The following publications also contributed to the development of the CHAP methodology:

Papers

Rao, S., Klesse, G., Stansfeld, P. J., Tucker, S. J., & Sansom, M. S. (2017). A BEST example of channel structure annotation by molecular simulation. Channels, 24, 1-7.

Trick, J. L., Chelvaniththilan, S., Klesse, G., Aryal, P., Wallace, E. J., Tucker, S. J., & Sansom, M. S. (2016). Functional annotation of ion channel structures by molecular simulation. Structure, 24(12), 2207-2216.

Posters

Klesse, G., Rao, S., Stansfeld, P. J., Sansom, M. S., & Tucker, S. J. (2018). A Newly Available Tool for Functional Annotation of Ion Channel Structures Based on Molecular Dynamics Simulations. Biophysical Journal, 114(3), 134a.

Rao, S., Klesse, G., Tucker, S. J., & Sansom, M. S. (2018). Hydrophobic Gating: Examination of Recent Ion Channel Structures. Biophysical Journal, 114(3), 134a.
